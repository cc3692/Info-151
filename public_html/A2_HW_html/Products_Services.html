Rentals

We offer rentals of all kinds of books. You can rent from textbooks to fictional books. There are no limit to how many you want to rent. We would love for you to continue your studies and leisurely activity of reading.
Rental prices are based on the type of books you are renting. There is a limit of a semester for textbooks and two months for leisure books. Extensions may be negotiated base on availability and rental standing for borrowers.

Previews

Want to get started on reading but don't know what to read? Want to see if the book is worth the money you are going to spend on it? Want to know what the some of the scenes of the book are like?
We offer you the choice of previewing a book before you buy the full copy. Every preview will contain a short summary of the book and different parts of the book that will definitely get you hooked on to the book. Previews are free to download, limited to twice a week.

Buying

Book readers all around the world will love our newest service of buying books. We deliver internationally of books of all sorts. There are new books, second hand books in need of new home, and much much more!
Buying books will give you the feeling of ownership of one of the greatest object on Earth. Books store knowledge and imagination. Free shipping is included once you buy more than three books of any mix.
